JKKNIU Skill Development Club (JKKNIU-SDC)

Overview
Mission
The mission of the club is to make the students aware to be a resource and provide them programs for their development of skills. JKKNIU-SDC wishes to produce adept and expert human resources.

Vision
JKKNIU-SDC’s vision is to create skill development movement not only in JKKNIU but also around the country to transform the young generation into human resources for contributing to the development of the country.

JKKNIU-SDC plans to develop the students as planet standard as an assisting organization of JKKNIU. It believes that it can make better things and bring better days for the young generation.

Objectives and Activities
The objectives of the club shall be to provide members facilities, opportunities and training for their skills development.

JKNNIU-SDC maintains the following activities to accomplish its mission:
- Diplomacy and Negotiations
- Teamwork, Networking and Social Relation
- Workshop on Computer Skills
- Seminar and Conferences
- Spoken English and Presentation
- Problem Solving
- Leadership, Training and Practice
- Workshop on Current Problems
- CV/Resume Making
- Arranging Competitions
- Counseling on Career
