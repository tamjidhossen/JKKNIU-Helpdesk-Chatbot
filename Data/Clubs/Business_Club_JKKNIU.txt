The
Business Club, JKKNIU is a student-run professional organization at Jatiya Kabi Kazi Nazrul Islam University (JKKNIU) dedicated to fostering entrepreneurship and leadership. Operating under the motto "Where every idea finds a home," it bridges the gap between academic theory and real-world corporate practice. 
Core Programs and Activities (2025–2026)
The club remains highly active with events designed to enhance student employability and business acumen: 

    Executive Business Citizen (EBC) Program: A selective hands-on leadership track where members work in executive teams on real-world projects and marketing campaigns.
    Talent Hunt 2025: A major competition featuring segments in Business Pitching, CV Creation, Graphics Design, and Professional Email Writing.
    Global Policy & Environment Conclave 2026: In early 2026, the club co-organized this international training platform at JKKNIU, focusing on green education, climate finance, and strategic communication.
    Career Webinars: Regularly hosts sessions like "The Art of Getting Hired" and "Road to Corporate" featuring industry experts from companies such as ACI Motors.
