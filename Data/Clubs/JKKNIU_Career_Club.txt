JKKNIU Career Club (JKKNIUCC)

Overview
JKKNIU Career Club (JKKNIUCC) is a career oriented club of Jatiya Kabi Kazi Nazrul Islam University established with motives to help JKKNIUians become more career conscious, prepare them as strong competitors for different competitive career opportunities and reach the ultimate peak of success. JKKNIUCC is established on 08 February, 2018 to represent student's excellence & co-curricular activities to develop the experience of them both in & out of the classroom as well as to smooth the path of their career. The association will act on student views & concern; elevate, co-ordinate & operate student activities and enhance channels of communication between student, faculty, administration, alumni, university community, the corporate world & workplace internationally.

The aim of JKKNIUCC is to arrange programs like career based seminars, regular career festivals, skill development workshops, higher study guideline seminars, interactive idea sharing sessions, work in different self-development programs, leadership skill trainings and much more which will be conducted by nation-famous speakers, trainers, motivators and our successful and bright alumni with supervision of our respected teachers.

Mission
• To create career consciousness
• To introduce its Members with economy’s different skills requirements
• To enhance skill and knowledge of its Members
• To instil and/or rejuvenate ethical value among its Members

Vision
JKKNIUCC’s vision is to create a career movement not only in the Jatiya Kabi Kazi Nazrul University's students but also around the country to transform the young generation in an expert & adept human resource to contribute to the economy of Bangladesh.
