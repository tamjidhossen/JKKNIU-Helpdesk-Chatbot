The Rotaract Club of Jatiya Kabi Kazi Nazrul Islam University (RAC-JKKNIU) is a campus-based youth service organization established on December 27, 2020. Sponsored by Rotary International, it focuses on developing leadership and professional skills while serving the community through fellowship. 
Recent and Ongoing Activities (2025–2026) 
The club has been highly active in early 2026 with career-focused and intra-club development programs: 

    Road to Study Abroad (January 12, 2026): A flagship career and education guidance seminar held at the Nobojug Seminar Hall, offering expert mentorship and application guidance for international education.
    Impact Ignite (January 2026): An intra-club excellence competition designed to foster leadership and innovation. It includes a series of "Ignite Challenges" where members identify community problems and develop practical solutions.
    Critical Thinking & Teamwork Session (January 1, 2026): A professional development workshop conducted by Sumaya Safat to equip members with strategic problem-solving tools.
    Graphic Design with AI Workshop (December 2025): A two-day intensive workshop in collaboration with NextGen ITES exploring AI's role in modern design.
