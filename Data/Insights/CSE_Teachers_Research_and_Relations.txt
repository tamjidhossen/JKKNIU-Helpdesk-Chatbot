CSE Teachers Research Insights and Complex Relations

This file maps the research landscape and collaborations within the CSE department at JKKNIU.

1. Research Clusters (Collaborative Themes)
- Digital Signal Processing (DSP) & Bio-Signals:
  * Key Figures: Sujan Ali, Jannatul Ferdous, Habiba Sultana, Mijanur Rahman, Kazi Mahmudul Hassan.
  * Focus: Neural Signal Processing, BCI, EEG analysis, Neuro-robotics.
- Health Informatics & Telemedicine:
  * Key Figures: Uzzal Kumar Prodhan, Subrata Kumar Das, Mijanur Rahman, Tushar Kanti Saha.
  * Focus: Remote healthcare, Distributed patient database management, COVID-19 pattern analysis.
- Cyber Security & Data Privacy:
  * Key Figures: Tushar Kanti Saha, Subrata Kumar Das, Mahbubun Nahar, A. H. M. Kamal.
  * Focus: Steganography (DNA & Image), Cryptography (Cloud security), Watermarking patient data.
- AI, Machine Learning & Computer Vision:
  * Key Figures: A. H. M. Kamal, Subrata Das, Mijanur Rahman, Mahnuma Rinty, Rubya Shaharin.
  * Focus: OCR (Image text extraction), Hand gesture recognition, Machine learning for healthcare.

- Shared Research Projects: Dr. Mijanur Rahman and Mahnuma Rinty have several joint publications on speech segmentation and OCR. Uzzal Prodhan and Tushar Saha collaborate frequently on telemedicine papers.
- Inter-Departmental Links: Dr. Kamal serves as Dean for the entire Faculty of Science and Engineering, linking CSE with EEE, ESE, and Statistics.
