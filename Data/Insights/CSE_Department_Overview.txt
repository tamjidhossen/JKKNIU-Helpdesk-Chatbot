Degrees Offered by CSE Department at JKKNIU
The Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at JKKNIU offers the following academic degrees: Bachelor of Science in Engineering (B.Sc. Engg.), Master of Science (MS), Master of Science in Engineering (M.Sc. Engg.), Master of Philosophy (M.Phil.), and Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.). The department provides education in core areas like AI, networking, and software engineering.

Postgraduate Admission (M.Sc. Engg.) in CSE at JKKNIU
The CSE department at JKKNIU offers a Master of Science (M.S./M.Sc. Engg.) program spanning one year and six months across three semesters. Eligible applicants must hold a four-year B.Sc. (Honors/Engg.) degree in CSE, EEE, IT, ICT, ECE, or ETE from a public university with a minimum CGPA of 3.00. The admission process involves a written or oral examination.
