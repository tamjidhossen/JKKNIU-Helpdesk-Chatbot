Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering (EEE) - Jatiya Kabi Kazi Nazrul Islam University (JKKNIU)

About the Department:
Electrical and Electronic Engineering (EEE) is a key technological department at JKKNIU with a significant socio-economic impact. The discipline covers a wide range of fields including:
- Electric machines and power systems
- Image and signal processing
- Biomedical engineering
- Information technologies
- Electronics
- Wireless and mobile communication
- Optical communication
- Control systems
- Machine learning systems

The department emphasizes interdisciplinary research and the realization of new technologies. It is known for enrolling top-quality students and began its operations in 2011.

Academic Programs:
- Undergraduate Enrollment: Generally 40 students per year.
- Degree Offered: B.Sc. in Electrical and Electronic Engineering (4-year program, 8 semesters).
- Focus: In the final three terms, students engage in specialized project and thesis work to build professional skills.

Vision:
To be a premier and value-based department in Bangladesh, committed to excellence by preparing students as successful Electrical and Electronic engineers who are efficient, knowledgeable, self-motivated, and dedicated to their profession.

Mission:
- Offer quality education in electrical and electronic engineering.
- Provide state-of-the-art resources for teaching, learning, research, and development.
- Bridge the gap between industry and academia through relevant curricula.
- Enhance creative talents of students and faculty.
- Develop skills to solve complex technological problems and promote multidisciplinary activities.
- Inculcate moral and ethical values.
- Prepare students for global competitiveness and impactful research.
- Develop genuine and dedicated electrical engineers.
