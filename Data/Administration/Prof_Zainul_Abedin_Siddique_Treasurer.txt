Treasurer: Zainul Abedin Siddique

Dr. Zainul Abedin Siddique serves as the Treasurer of Jatiya Kabi Kazi Nazrul Islam University (JKKNIU), located in Trishal, Mymensingh-2224. He is also a Professor in the Department of Chemistry, Faculty of Science, at Chittagong University, Chittagong-4331, Bangladesh.

Academic and Professional Background
Dr. Siddique holds a PhD from Osaka University and a PD from Kyoto University. His academic career spans significant roles in teaching and administration, contributing to his current leadership position at JKKNIU.

Message from the Treasurer
"I am greatly delighted to be a member of Jatiya Kabi Kazi Nazrul Islam University (JKKNIU) family. This University endeavors to uphold the realm of creations of our national poet Kazi Nazrul Islam. JKKNIU has been striving to achieve the goal in disseminating the tertiary education since its inception in 2006. It’s a crying need to develop and nurture a quality based higher education and research enabling the individuals to be a noble human being as well as a global citizen for an anti-discriminatory and sustainable society. The recent July 36-2024 movement has signaled us to unveil a new horizon encompassing the nation building aspiration transfused with humanity, uprightness and patriotism. It’s my privilege to be engaged in streamlining the stakeholders including our bright students, qualified faculties, dedicated researchers and affiliated international collaborators. Under the prevailing scenario, let us devote ourselves in the struggles reshaping our academic excellency encircling global knowledge and novelty of research."
