Physical Education
Established: 2009-12-15
Address: Nama Para Battala, Trishal, Mymensingh

About:
The Physical Education department of JKKNIU was established for the physical and mental development of students. The department organizes co-curricular activities such as football, cricket, volleyball, badminton, and table tennis. It also conducts yearly training programs to enhance players' skills and arranges inter-departmental and inter-university sports competitions.
