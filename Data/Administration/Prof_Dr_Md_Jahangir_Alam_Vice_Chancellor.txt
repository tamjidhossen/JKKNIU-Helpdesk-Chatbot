Biography of Professor Dr. Md. Jahangir Alam\n\nProfessor Dr. Md. Jahangir Alam currently serves as the Vice-Chancellor of Jatiya Kabi Kazi Nazrul Islam University (JKKNIU). Before assuming this leadership role on September 19, 2024, he was a Professor in the Department of Sociology at the University of Dhaka.\n\nAcademic Background\nPh.D. in Rural and Regional Development Planning: Asian Institute of Technology (AIT), Bangkok, Thailand (2012).\nMaster of Science in Urban Planning, Land, and Housing Development: Asian Institute of Technology (AIT), Bangkok, Thailand (2000).\nMaster of Social Science (MSS) in Sociology: University of Chittagong, Bangladesh (1993).\nBachelor of Social Science (BSS) in Sociology: University of Chittagong, Bangladesh (1992).\n\nResearch and Expertise\nProfessor Alam’s teaching and research areas focus on social structure, social problems, urban environment, and urban land management. He has published over 22 peer-reviewed articles in international journals. His notable research includes studies on:\nImpacts of environmental laws on land development in Dhaka City.\nHealth awareness and knowledge among slum dwellers.\nSpatial development of Dhaka and its impacts on the built environment.\nThe challenges of security and tenure for squatters.\n\nAwards and Fellowships\nAIT Ph.D. Fellowship (2008): Awarded by the Asian Institute of Technology for doctoral studies in Thailand.\nDAAD Scholarship (1998): Awarded by the German Academic Exchange Service for Master's studies at AIT.\n\nMessage from the Vice-Chancellor\nAs Vice-Chancellor, Professor Dr. Md. Jahangir Alam emphasizes that JKKNIU proudly carries the spirit of the "Rebel Poet," Kazi Nazrul Islam. Guided by Nazrul's legacy of justice, resistance to oppression, and fearless creativity, the university strives to:\nPreserve Cultural Heritage: Uphold human dignity and promote progressive thought while nurturing inquisitive scholars and compassionate leaders.\nFoster Innovation: Aim beyond classroom instruction to generate knowledge that serves nation-building and the global community.\nPromote Inclusivity: Build a community that values intellectual rigor, research excellence, and diversity to establish JKKNIU as a center of academic distinction.\nProfessor Alam remains committed to supporting JKKNIU’s journey of discovery and positive societal transformation, encouraging everyone to let Nazrul's fearless spirit inspire their pursuit of excellence.\n\nContact Information\nTo facilitate communication for students and visitors, Jatiya Kabi Kazi Nazrul Islam University (JKKNIU) provides multiple contact channels for academic and administrative inquiries.
