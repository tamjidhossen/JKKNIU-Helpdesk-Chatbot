Announcement: Master's (MIS) Students Fellowship 2025-2026
Date: January 09, 2026
Ref. No: JKKNIU/Misc/01/2026

Details:
Applications are invited from Master's (MIS) level students at JKKNIU for the research fellowship for the 2025-2026 financial year.

Application Deadline: January 26, 2026

Fellowship Benefits:
- Monthly Stipend: BDT 5,000/-

Fellowship Duration:
- Master's by Research: Maximum 2 years.
- Master's by Mixed Mode: Maximum 6 months.

Eligibility & Requirements:
- Must be a Master's (MIS) level student at JKKNIU.
- Application must include recommendations from the Supervisor, Department Head, and the concerned committee.
- Required documents include academic records and research committee approvals.

Application Submission:
Interested students must submit their applications to the Director, Research and Extension Center, Jatiya Kabi Kazi Nazrul Islam University.
