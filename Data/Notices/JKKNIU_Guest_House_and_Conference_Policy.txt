JKKNIU Guest House Usage Policy & Virtual Conference Room Guidelines

Institution: Jatiya Kabi Kazi Nazrul Islam University, Trishal, Mymensingh-2220.

---
### Part 1: Guest House Usage Policy

1. Purpose and Scope
- Primary Use: Accommodation for university teachers and officials.
- Priority: University business takes priority over all other stays.
- Eligible Purposes: Higher education, training, seminars, workshops, conferences, medical needs, official duties, research, and personal reasons.

2. Eligibility for Accommodation
- Teachers and officials of the university.
- Family members of teachers/officials (if accompanied by the employee).
- External members of university committees (Syndicate, FC, Works Committee, etc.).

3. Allocation Rules
- Basis: "First Come, First Served".
- Priority Override: Teachers/officials summoning for urgent university work by the Vice-Chancellor get priority.
- Booking: Requires prior permission from Administration/Estate Office via prescribed form.
- Non-Transferable: Bookings cannot be transferred to another person.

4. Booking Procedure
- Lead Time: Submit application at least 3 days in advance (1 day for emergencies).
- Online Option: Forms can be filled online and emailed to Estate Office/Dhaka Guest House.
- Check-in/Out:
  - Must sign register upon arrival.
  - Keys must be returned upon checkout.
  - Standard Day: 12:00 PM to 12:00 PM (24 hours = 1 day).

5. Fee Structure (Daily Rates)

A. Dhaka Guest House
- Official Use:
  - Non-AC Single: 150/-
  - Non-AC Double (Per Bed): 100/-
  - AC Single: 250/-
- Personal Use:
  - Non-AC Single: 250/-
  - Non-AC Double (Per Bed): 200/-
  - AC Single: 500/-

B. Newly Built Campus Guest House
- Official Use:
  - AC (VIP) Single: 200/-
  - AC Double (Per Bed): 150/-
- Personal Use:
  - AC (VIP) Single: 300/-
  - AC Double (Per Bed): 200/-

C. Old Campus Guest House
- Rate: 100/- per room.

*Note: Day use (without overnight stay) is charged at full daily rate.*

6. Stay Limits and Cancellations
- Maximum Duration: 4 days (Extensions require Treasurer approval in special cases).
- Extension Request: Must be made 24 hours in advance via staff to Estate Office.
- Cancellation: Must be reported at least 24 hours in advance.

7. Amenities and Restrictions
- Amenities: Drawing room (sofa/TV), cooking facilities/cook, one daily Bengali newspaper.
- Visitors: Allowed until 11:00 PM.
- Personal Usage Limit: Max 2 times per month per person.
