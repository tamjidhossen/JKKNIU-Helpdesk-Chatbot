Condolence Notice: Passing of Begum Khaleda Zia
Published Date: December 30, 2025
Updated Date: January 08, 2026

Details:
The JKKNIU family expresses deep sorrow and grief at the passing of Begum Khaleda Zia, Chairperson of the Bangladesh Nationalist Party (BNP) and former Prime Minister of Bangladesh.

She passed away at 3:00 PM on Tuesday, December 30, 2025, while undergoing treatment at Evercare Hospital in Dhaka. She had been admitted on November 20, 2025, suffering from multiple ailments including arthritis, heart disease, and kidney complications.

Message from Vice-Chancellor:
The Honorable Vice-Chancellor, Professor Dr. Md. Jahangir Alam, expressed that her death is an irreparable loss for the nation, noting her legacy as the first female Prime Minister of Bangladesh and her contributions to the country's democratic development.
