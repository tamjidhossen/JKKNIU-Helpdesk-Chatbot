Office Order: VC's Travel Sanction and Acting VC Arrangement
Date: December 17, 2025
Reference: Office Order No. 37.00.0000.079.25.131.18- 517

Details:
The Vice Chancellor of Jatiya Kabi Kazi Nazrul Islam University (JKKNIU), Professor Dr. Md. Jahangir Alam, has been granted leave to travel to Australia for personal purposes.

Duration: 17 days, from December 18, 2025, to January 03, 2026.

Acting VC Arrangement:
In the absence of the Vice Chancellor, Dr. Zainul Abedin Siddique (Treasurer of JKKNIU) will carry out the routine functions of the Vice Chancellor.

Terms:
- All expenses related to the travel will be borne by the Vice Chancellor.
- No financial involvement from the Government of Bangladesh or the University.
- The Vice Chancellor will resume duties and inform the Ministry upon return.
