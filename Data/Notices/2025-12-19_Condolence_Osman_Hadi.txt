Condolence Notice: Passing of Osman Hadi
Published Date: December 19, 2025
Updated Date: January 08, 2026

Details:
The JKKNIU family expresses profound grief and sorrow at the sad demise of Osman Hadi 

He passed away on Thursday, December 18, 2025, at 9:45 PM while undergoing treatment at Singapore General Hospital. He had been critically injured in a assasination attack on December 12, 2025, and was later airlifted to Singapore for advanced treatment.

Message from Vice-Chancellor:
The Honorable Vice-Chancellor, Professor Dr. Md. Jahangir Alam, on behalf of the university, conveyed deep condolences to the bereaved family and demanded a swift, impartial, and exemplary trial for this heinous murder.
